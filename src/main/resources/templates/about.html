How SleepyTales Works

Create

Write your story, add images, and craft interactive questions that engage readers and enhance comprehension. Our intuitive story editor makes it easy for creators of all experience levels.

Submit

Share your creation with our community moderation team. Our collaborative approach ensures stories are engaging, appropriate, and aligned with our community values.

Publish

Once approved, your story becomes available in our community library, where readers worldwide can discover, enjoy, and interact with your creation.

Our Core Values

Creativity

We celebrate imagination and original thinking. SleepyTales provides tools and a platform for creative expression, encouraging storytellers to explore new ideas and narrative approaches.

Community

We believe in the power of collective creativity. Our collaborative environment fosters supportive connections between storytellers, readers, educators, and story enthusiasts.

Education

Stories can be powerful teaching tools. We emphasize narratives that engage readers while imparting valuable knowledge, social-emotional skills, and cultural understanding.

Inclusivity

We welcome diverse voices, perspectives, and storytelling traditions from around the world. Our platform aims to represent the rich tapestry of human experience through stories that resonate across cultures.

Safety

We maintain a secure environment where all content is carefully reviewed. Our moderation process ensures that stories are appropriate and uphold our community standards.

Empathy

Stories help us understand different perspectives and experiences. We value narratives that cultivate empathy, compassion, and emotional intelligence in readers of all ages.

Community Guidelines

To maintain a positive and enriching environment, we ask all contributors to follow these guidelines:

  1. Quality Content: Stories should be well-crafted with attention to narrative flow, character development, and engaging elements that captivate readers.
  2. Positive Values: Content should promote values such as empathy, kindness, curiosity, resilience, and respect for diversity.
  3. Inclusive Representation: We encourage diverse characters and perspectives that authentically reflect our global community.
  4. Educational Merit: The best stories not only entertain but also teach valuable lessons or inspire curiosity about the world.
  5. Original Content: All submissions should be original work or properly attributed if adapting traditional tales in the public domain.
  6. Constructive Feedback: When interacting with other community members, provide thoughtful, respectful feedback that helps improve storytelling craft.
